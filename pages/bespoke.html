The Five Steps to Your Masterpiece

1

The Consultation

We discuss your needs, lifestyle, and preferences. We select the finest fabrics, linings, and specific garment details.

2

Master Measurement

Our tailor takes over 30 precise body measurements and records your posture to ensure a flawless and unique fit.

3

The Basting Fitting

The garment is temporarily constructed ("basted"). You try it on for the first time, and we make precise adjustments to the silhouette.

4

Final Adjustments

The suit is finished, and you return for a final check. Any minor tweaks are handled to perfect the movement and drape.

5

Handover

Your finished, fully bespoke RIGO WEAR garment is ready for you to take home. Welcome to the world of true custom luxury.
