RIGO WEAR ADMIN MANUAL

The complete guide to managing your luxury tailor CMS.

1. The Media Library

The Media Library is the heart of your product creation process. All images must be securely uploaded to the Supabase Cloud Storage before they can be assigned to suits or blogs.

How to Upload Images

  • Navigate to the Media Library tab in the sidebar.
  • Click the large dashed upload zone, or Drag and Drop images from your computer directly into the box.
  • Wait for the Uploading... progress tracker to turn green for each file.
PRO TIP: Compress your images before uploading! Huge files make the website load slowly for your customers. Aim for files around 500KB to 1MB max.

Creating a Product from an Image

To swiftly create a new inventory item:

  • Click on any image sitting inside the Media Library grid.
  • A golden modal will pop up. Enter the Product Name, Pricing, Category, and Description.
  • Hit save, and the product instantly goes live on your shopping page!

2. Managing Inventory

The Inventory table gives you a bird's-eye view of everything you are selling, letting you perform quick adjustments in real-time.

PRICING & CLASSIFICATIONS: When setting prices for your suits, keep in mind the three official RIGO WEAR bespoke classifications:
  • Standard/Essential: KES 8,000 - 12,000
  • Classic Executive: KES 13,000 - 17,000
  • Premium Signature: KES 18,000+
The pricing you set here directly informs the customer of the suit's luxury tier.

Editing Existing Products

  • Locate the product row in the table (use the Search Bar if needed).
  • Click the ✏️ Pencil Button in the Actions column.
  • The Edit Modal will appear over the screen. Update the Category, Name, Prices, or Description, then click Save Changes.

Quick Price Changes

You don't need to open the modal for a simple price change! Just type the new price into the input box on the table row and click the 💾 Floppy Disk Button to lock it in.

Stock Statuses

Is an item out of fabric? Simply click the green In Stock badge. It will instantly flip to a red Out of Stock badge, alerting your customers.

3. The Signature Collection

The "Signature Collection" on the front page of your website highlights your most premium or best-selling garments. You control what appears here.

Featuring a Product

  • In the Manage Inventory tab, find the ⭐ Star Button under the Actions column.
  • If the Star is Golden Filled, it is actively featured on the Home Page!
  • If the Star is an Empty Outline, it is just a normal shop item. Click it to instantly promote it to Signature status.

4. Editorials & Insights

The "Home Page CMS" tab allows you to write luxury editorial posts that appear at the bottom of the home page under "Latest Insights".

Creating an Article

  • Fill out the Title and the bold Author Name.
  • Write a spicy Short Preview (this goes on the home page card) and the full Main Story Content (what they read when they click).
  • Click the 🖼️ Choose from Gallery button. This opens a mini version of your Media Library! Click any image to set it as the article's cover photo.
  • Hit Publish to Website to broadcast your thoughts.
PRO TIP: Your published blogs will appear at the bottom of the Home Page CMS tab. You can click the Trash Can icon there to delete old articles.

5. Client Orders

The system automatically captures orders when customers click "Checkout on WhatsApp" or complete transactions. Details regarding their requested fit, fabrics, and quantities are tracked here so you never lose contact strings.
